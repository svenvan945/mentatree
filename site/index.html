The loop

It grades itself.

Every prediction gets a probability and a review date. When the date comes, mentatree resurfaces it, you mark what happened, and it scores your calibration. Are your 70%s really 70%?

your calibration so far [50-60) 3/7 came true [70-80) 6/8 came true [90-100] 4/4 came true brier 0.121 · 37 resolved
